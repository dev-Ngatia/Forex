Indicator Reference Guide

Indicators Ideal Range Per Scenario
  • EMA 9/21
  • EMA 50/200
 Trend Confirmation:
9 > 21 > 50 > 200 = Uptrend
9 < 21 < 50 < 200 = Downtrend
  • RSI
 Overbought/Oversold:
0-30: Oversold (Buy Signal)
70-100: Overbought (Sell Signal)
30-70: Neutral Zone
  • MACD
 Signal & Histogram:
MACD > Signal = Bullish
MACD < Signal = Bearish
Histogram shows momentum
  • Stochastic
 Fast Mean Reversion:
0-20: Oversold (Buy)
80-100: Overbought (Sell)
K > D = Bullish Crossover
  • Bollinger Bands
 Volatility & Support:
Price at Upper Band = Resistance
Price at Lower Band = Support
Squeeze = Low Volatility
  • ATR
 Volatility Meter:
High ATR = High Volatility (Risk)
Low ATR = Low Volatility (Range)
Set Stop Loss = 2x ATR
  • VWAP
 Intraday Support/Resistance:
Price > VWAP = Bullish
Price < VWAP = Bearish
Retest = Entry Point
  • Fibonacci Levels
 Support & Resistance Zones:
38.2%, 50%, 61.8% = Key Reversals
